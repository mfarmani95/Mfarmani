Key Findings from Our Study

1. Influence of Soil Hydraulic Parameterization: The Brooks-Corey/Clapp-Hornberger (CH) model enhances surface moisture but shortens root zone persistence ...

2. Role of Soil Macropores: Macropores accelerate infiltration, reducing surface moisture retention while affecting deeper SMM dynamics ...

3. Impact of Surface Ponding: A higher ponding threshold enhances infiltration opportunities, extending long-term memory ...
